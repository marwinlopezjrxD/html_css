=== Main Dishes ===

Adobo

Tender meat in savory-tangy soy-vinegar sauce

The quintessential Filipino comfort food. This dish features tender meat (usually pork or chicken) slow-cooked and simmered in a savory, tangy sauce made from soy sauce, vinegar, crushed garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. It's rich, deeply flavorful, and perfectly paired with a mountain of white rice.

Adobong Tuyo

Dry adobo fried in its own fat

This is a "dry" version of the classic adobo, where the meat is simmered in the adobo marinade until the sauce reduces completely and the meat fries in its own rendered fat. The result is an intensely flavorful, concentrated savory and salty bite, often a beloved Filipino breakfast staple served with fried rice and egg.

Bicol Express

Spicy pork stew with coconut milk

A famous dish from the Bicol region, known for its fiery kick and creamy texture. It's a hearty stew made with pork, long green chilies (siling haba), and a generous amount of rich coconut milk, often seasoned with shrimp paste (bagoong). It's a spicy, savory, and rich main dish that guarantees you'll need extra rice!

=== Side Dishes ===

Tomatoes with Onions

Fresh tomatoes + red onions + honey-vinegar

A bright and crisp side salad using the freshest tomatoes and thinly sliced red onions. The unique dressing is a simple blend of tangy apple cider vinegar, a touch of honey for balance, and salt to taste. (Ang sarap nito promise!)

Cucumber Salad

Cool, crisp, refreshing vinegar-based salad

A classic, crisp, and refreshing side dish that brings a burst of cool flavor to any meal. This salad is light and simple, focusing on the fresh texture of thinly sliced cucumbers and red onions. It’s typically tossed in a bright vinegar-based dressing lightly sweetened and seasoned with savory notes of fresh dill and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.

Dessert

Bananaque

Caramelized saba bananas on a stick

A popular Filipino street food and dessert made of saba bananas skewered on a bamboo stick, coated generously in brown sugar, and then deep-fried until the sugar caramelizes into a crispy, golden-brown glaze.

Turon

Crispy fried banana spring rolls

Sweet, deep-fried spring rolls. Slices of saba banana (sometimes with a slice of jackfruit) are wrapped in a thin lumpia wrapper, rolled in brown sugar, and fried until golden, crunchy, and caramelized.

Alcoholic Beverages

Gin & Tonic

Crisp gin with premium tonic

A universally popular, crisp, and clean highball cocktail. It expertly balances the botanical notes of gin with the bright, effervescent bitterness of premium tonic water, always served over ice with a fresh lime wedge.

Margarita

Tequila, lime, salt-rimmed perfection

A vibrant and zesty classic cocktail. The perfect blend of tequila, fresh lime juice, and orange liqueur (like Cointreau), served chilled in a salt-rimmed glass. It delivers a refreshing balance of sweet, sour, and savory flavors.

Mojito

Mint, lime, rum, and bubbles

A refreshing and invigorating Cuban classic. This cocktail combines the sweet freshness of muddled mint and lime with rum, sugar, and soda water, creating the perfect light and bubbly drink for a warm day.
